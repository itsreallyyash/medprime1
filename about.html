About Us

At a Glance

With over 50 years of experience in the pharmaceutical industry, we have built a diverse and robust portfolio. Our journey began with retail pharmacy stores under the names "Laxmi Medical Stores," "Mandeo Medico," and "Nakoda Medical."

Recognizing the potential for growth, we launched "Labdhi Lifesciences," a company dedicated to the distribution of generic medications. Labdhi Lifesciences offers an extensive range of over 400 products, catering to various sectors such as orthopaedics, dermatology, over-the-counter (OTC), diabetes care, and respiratory care products.

In our ongoing pursuit of expansion and innovation, we are proud to introduce "MedPrime International." This latest venture focuses on merchant exporting and contract manufacturing, reinforcing our presence in the global pharmaceutical market.
